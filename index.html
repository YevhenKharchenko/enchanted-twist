Cards. Images. Questions. Shifts.

Enchanted Twist is a visual metaphor puzzle system.

You explore a deck of illustrated tiles, each evoking stories, choices, and inner landscapes.
The platform helps you or your group reflect through imagery, unlock insight, and approach dilemmas from new angles.

It’s not fortune-telling. It’s pattern-seeing.

52 visual metaphors. No words. No rules.

Each deck includes:

  • Symbolic illustrations

  • Context cards (mood, polarity, archetype)

  • Digital shuffling and spread placement

  • Custom layouts for solo or group use

Designed by artists, psychologists, and storytellers.

Open. Choose. Ask. Reflect.

You can:

  • • Pick a random card before decision-making

  • • Invite a group to react to the same symbol

  • • Map your inner tension using three tiles

  • • Break team stagnation with metaphor circles

  • • Use it in workshops, retreats, or one-on-one coaching

Visual language bypasses defense. Insight flows faster.

Built for intuition. Powered by design

  • Live collaboration mode (shared boards)
